# Introduction

This is a transcript of TNA, SP 28/140/17, the establishment and rates of pay of the Earl of Essex's army. Although dated 1645, it seems to refer to 1642 as the size of a troop of harquebusiers was increased later.

The establishment for foot companies does not give the number of ordinary soldiers or their rate of pay.

## Transcription conventions

- Original spelling is retained except that i/j and u/v are silently modernized where used interchangeably, and ff at the start of a word is silently modernized to F.
- All original puncuation is retained. No punctuation has been added.
- Abbreviations are expanded in square brackets except that & is retained and not expanded.
- Insertions: [^inserted text^].
- Page breaks: [f. 399r]
- Headings for table columns in square brackets are editorial additions for clarity.
- There is no deleted text in the original manuscript.
