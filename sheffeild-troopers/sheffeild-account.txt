The[se] are to Certifie whome it may Concerne that the bearer here of Collonell James Shefeild was in actuall service as Captaine and Collonell of A regim[en]t of horse in the late Lord Gen[era]ll the Earle of Essex army vi[delice]t as Captaine from the 22th of July 1642 to the 5th of May 1645 & as Collonell from 17 July 1643 to the s[ai]d 5th of May 1645 duringe w[hi]ch time as he was Captaine he kept 6 leere horses for the service of the state & behaved himselfe fathfully & valiantly like a soldier. Dat[ed]

W[illiam] Balfour

Coll[onel] James Sheffield was in actuall service as is above written and as is comissions will (I doubt not) declare

Ph[ilip] Skippon
